J-Doody

J-Doody is a dynamic EDM artist known for his infectious beats and electrifying performances. His music blends pulsating rhythms with catchy melodies, creating an exhilarating fusion of dancefloor anthems and emotive soundscapes. With a knack for pushing boundaries and crafting tracks that resonate with energy and emotion, J-Doody captivates audiences worldwide with his signature style and relentless passion for electronic music.



GypsyJoeLee

GypsyJoeLee is a soulful folk artist whose music weaves heartfelt storytelling with acoustic melodies. With a voice that carries warmth and authenticity, GypsyJoeLee's songs resonate with themes of love, life's journeys, and the beauty of simplicity. His music transports listeners to a place where introspection meets melody, creating a captivating and memorable folk experience.
